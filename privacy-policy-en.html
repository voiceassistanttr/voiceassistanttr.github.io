IMPORTANT: WE COLLECT NO DATA

Android Voice Assistant app does not collect, store, or send any personal data to our servers. All operations are performed solely on your device.

1. About the App

Android Voice Assistant is designed for all users seeking convenience in smartphone usage. The app is developed with accessibility design principles, especially for users with visual difficulties, motor skill challenges, or technology adaptation needs.

2. System Requirements

  • Minimum Android version: Android 5.0 (API 21)
  • Target Android version: Android 14 (API 34)
  • RAM: At least 1GB recommended
  • Storage: 50MB free space
  • Age rating: 4+ (suitable for everyone)

3. Data Collection - NO DATA IS COLLECTED

This app:

  • Collects no personal data
  • Sends no information over the internet
  • Saves no user data
  • Performs no analytics tracking
  • Establishes no server connections

4. App Modules

4.1 Modules Running on Your Device

  • Phone: Uses your device's calling feature
  • SMS: Uses your device's messaging app
  • Contacts: Opens your device's contacts app
  • Alarm: Uses your device's alarm app
  • Reminder: Uses your device's reminder app
  • Notes: Saves notes only on your device
  • Camera: Opens your device's camera app
  • Music (Offline): Plays music files on your device

4.2 Third-Party Apps

  • WhatsApp: Opens WhatsApp app
  • Internet Search: Opens browser app
  • Music (Online): Opens music streaming apps
  • Radio: Opens radio apps
  • Mail: Opens email app
  • News: Opens news websites

5. Permissions

Permissions requested by our app and their purposes:

  • Microphone: Only to understand voice commands. No audio is recorded.
  • Contacts: Only to open contacts app. No contact information is read.
  • SMS: Only to open messaging app. No messages are read.
  • Phone: Only to make calls. No call logs are recorded.
  • Camera: Only to open camera app. No photos are processed.
  • Storage: Only to save app settings. No personal files are accessed.
  • Internet: Only for third-party apps. No connections to our servers.

6. Third-Party Apps

IMPORTANT: When our app opens other applications like WhatsApp, browser, or music apps, their own privacy policies apply. We are not responsible for these apps' data processing.

7. Data Security

  • Since we collect no data, there is no data security risk
  • All operations occur only on your device
  • Internet connection is used only for third-party apps
  • Since we have no servers, there is no data breach risk
